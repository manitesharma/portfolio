A SNEAK PEAK

Hi, I'm Manishanker! I'm an experience designer from Delhi India. I have a passion for Design, Technology and Business and you'll often find me at an intersection of these spaces. I've always been a creative person at heart, but through my different experiences over the time I've developed an intense curiosity for the way people interact with each other and the world around them, and how technology can help facilitate this intercommunication and existence.
I've spent the last few years of my life working with different startups building a cohesive portfolio of work, and an extensive skillset fueled by my curiosities in these spaces. I have an intense love for learning and strongly believe that learning as much as we can about the world around us can help facilitate interesting new experiences. Through my design thinking skills, I aim to help foster an environment where we democratize the use of technology while using it to better lives the world over. I love working with people - I've found immense value in working in interdisciplinary teams, and have arguably learned more doing so than in any other facet of my education.
